Our Story

Fashion Born from Pain, Transformed into Power

Glass Ink Haven was not created in comfort. It was created in the quiet — in the moments no one sees. In the nights where your chest feels heavy for no clear reason. In the silence after heartbreak. In the slow, painful process of becoming someone new after losing who you used to be.

This brand was not built from trends or aesthetics. It was built from emotion. From healing. From survival.

We believe clothing is more than fabric. It is a second skin — a form of expression for the feelings you cannot always explain. Every piece you wear should reflect something deeper — your softness, your strength, your scars, your growth.

Glass Ink Haven exists for the ones who feel deeply.

The overthinkers. The silent fighters. The ones who are rebuilding themselves quietly while the world keeps moving.

This is not just fashion.
This is therapy you can wear.

Glass Ink Haven was created during a deeply personal season — a time marked by emotional weight, inner battles, and a search for meaning. In that season, one truth became clear: there was no space in fashion for people who were healing. No brand that truly understood what it meant to feel everything… and still keep going.

So this became that space. A space where emotions are not hidden, but expressed. Where pain is not buried, but transformed into something meaningful. Where vulnerability is not weakness, but strength.

Our mission is simple, but deeply intentional:

  • To turn pain into something beautiful.
  • To give people a voice — even when they have no words.
  • To remind you that healing is not linear… but it is happening.

At the heart of Glass Ink Haven is faith, resilience, and growth. The belief that even in your lowest moments, you are not alone. That God is still present. That even when everything feels uncertain, something within you is still holding on.

Every hoodie, every tee, every piece you wear carries that message. We are not just a brand. We are a community. A community of people who feel deeply, love deeply, and are learning — day by day — how to heal.

Your story matters here. If you have a story, a feeling, or a moment you want to express, you can share it with us — and we will help you turn it into something meaningful. Something seen. Something that speaks.

Glass Ink Haven is more than clothing.
It is a sanctuary stitched into fabric.

You are welcome here — exactly as you are. ✨

Shipping & Returns

All items are made-to-order. This means your product is created specifically for you after you place an order.

Because of this, we do not accept returns or exchanges for:

  • Wrong size selected
  • Wrong color selected
  • Change of mind

However, if your item arrives damaged or there is a production error:

  • Contact us within 30 days of delivery
  • Send a clear photo of the issue
  • We will offer a free replacement or refund

No need to return the item.

If delivery fails due to incorrect address:

You may request a reprint (extra cost) or partial refund

Please note: Slight differences in print placement are normal and not considered defects.

Care Instructions

To keep your pieces looking fresh and lasting longer:

  • Wash inside-out
  • Use cold water
  • Do not bleach
  • Do not iron directly on the print
  • Air dry recommended

Treat it with care — it was made with meaning.

FAQ

1. How long does shipping take?

Production takes 2–5 business days. Shipping typically takes 5–10 business days depending on your location.

2. How is shipping calculated?

Shipping depends on your country and number of items. It is calculated automatically at checkout.

3. Do you ship worldwide?

Yes, we ship worldwide.

4. Can I return my item?

We do not accept returns for size or preference issues since items are made-to-order.

5. What if my item is damaged?

Contact us within 30 days with a photo and we will replace or refund it.

6. Can I customize or personalize a product?

Yes. Please contact us at glassinkhaven@gmail.com and we will assist you.

7. How can I track my order?

Once your order is shipped, you will receive a tracking link via email.
